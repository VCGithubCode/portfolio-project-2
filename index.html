Instructions for Using the Calculators

1. **How old am I?** Enter your date of birth, and the calculator will display your current age. If today is your birthday, it will wish you a Happy Birthday!


2. **What year was I born?** Enter your age, and the calculator will estimate the year you were born. It will provide options based on whether your birthday has occurred this year.


3. **How many days between?** Enter two dates to calculate the number of days between them. If you only enter the first date, the second will default to today's date.


Click on the "Age", "Birth Year", or "Date Difference" boxes (radio buttons) to select the calculator you want to use, and the appropriate fields will be displayed for you to input your data. Then, click the calculate button to see your results. You can then click on the recalculate button that appears if you wish to reenter your data.
